Water Footprint: Understanding Your Impact

A water footprint is the total volume of freshwater used by an individual or to produce the goods and services consumed by an individual, community, or business. Understanding your water footprint can help you make more sustainable choices in your daily life.

Our calculator helps you estimate your personal water footprint by considering various aspects of your water usage, including personal hygiene, outdoor usage, kitchen usage, and more.
